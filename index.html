Welcome to Tamagotcha!

Make sure to read the rules before getting started!

  • Name your pet!
  • Try to age your pet as far as you can!
  • Do not let hunger, boredom, or sleepiness
    reach 10, or it will die!
  • Click to decrease HUNGER by 1.
    BE CAREFUL, it'll increase BOREDOM by 1.
  • Click to decrease BOREDOM by 1.
    BE CAREFUL, it'll increase SLEEPINESS by 1.
  • Click to decrease SLEEPINESS by 1.
    BE CAREFUL, it'll increase HUNGER by 1.
  • Last but not least, HAVE FUN!
